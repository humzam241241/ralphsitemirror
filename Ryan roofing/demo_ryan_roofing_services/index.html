How does Ryan’s Roofing use AI in roofing projects?

We use AI tools for accurate estimating, drone inspections, and predictive maintenance alerts. This means faster service, fewer surprises, and roofs that last longer.

Do you serve both homes and businesses?

Yes. We provide full-service roofing for residential, commercial, and industrial properties throughout Durham Region and the Greater Toronto Area.

Are drone inspections safe and reliable?

Absolutely. Our drones provide precise visual data without risking safety on steep or damaged roofs, allowing for faster assessments and better accuracy.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Raffy’s Roofing is fully licensed, insured, and compliant with all Durham area regulations.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days, depending on size and weather.

What roofing materials do you use?

We work with top-rated materials from trusted brands to ensure long-term durability and curb appeal.

We offer emergency response for leaks and storm damage. Call or text right away — we’ll prioritize urgent repairs.

Always. We leave your yard and driveway spotless, magnet-sweeping for nails and hauling away all debris.

Some sample questions to ask Raffy

For this test, questions are limited to 20

We can train the AI Assistant according to your business information

🧱 1. Introduction & Identity Checks

Make sure Raffy introduces himself naturally, with personality.

  1. “Who are you?”

  2. “What’s your name again?”

  3. “Are you a real roofer or a bot?”

  4. “Where’s Ryan’s Roofing located?”

  5. “Do you actually climb roofs?”

  6. “What do you do?”

  7. “How long has Ryan’s Roofing been around?”

🧰 2. Service Knowledge Tests

These test if Raffy knows his roofing business — repairs, materials, and maintenance.

  1. “Do you fix roof leaks?”

  2. “Can you replace missing shingles?”

  3. “Do you do skylight installations?”

  4. “Can you work on metal roofs?”

  5. “Do you clean gutters?”

  6. “Do you handle emergency leaks?”

  7. “Do you offer attic insulation?”

  8. “How long does a roof replacement take?”

  9. “What roofing materials do you use?”

  10. “Can I get a free estimate?”

  11. “Do you work with insurance for storm damage?”

💬 3. Sales & Conversion Prompts

Check whether Raffy confidently steers the conversation toward booking, not just chatting.

  1. “Can I get a quote?”

  2. “How much would it cost to replace a roof?”

  3. “Do you offer financing or payment plans?”

  4. “Can I schedule someone to come check my roof?”

  5. “What’s your availability this week?”

  6. “Do you give discounts for referrals?”

  7. “I’m comparing prices — what makes Ryan’s Roofing better?”

🌦️ 4. Emergency & Urgent Scenarios

Raffy should stay calm, compassionate, and ready with action.

  1. “My roof’s leaking right now!”

  2. “My ceiling just started dripping!”

  3. “A tree branch hit my roof — what do I do?”

  4. “My shingles blew off in last night’s storm.”

  5. “Can you send someone tonight?”

  6. “Do you have 24/7 service?”

🪜 5. Humor & Personality Checks

Raffy should sound friendly and relatable — confident but never cocky.

  1. “Hey Raffy, are you afraid of heights?”

  2. “Do you wear one of those fancy tool belts?”

  3. “What’s the worst roof you’ve ever seen?”

  4. “You sound too confident — sure you’re not flirting?”

  5. “Tell me a roof joke.”

  6. “How’d you get the name Raffy?”

  7. “Do you get tired of talking about shingles all day?”

🧭 6. Objections & Hesitations

Test Raffy’s ability to handle price pushback and customer doubt with empathy and charm.

  1. “That sounds expensive.”

  2. “I might just wait until next year.”

  3. “Another company quoted me cheaper.”

  4. “I’m not sure I even need a repair.”

  5. “Can I just patch it myself?”

📋 7. Escalation & Boundary

Ensure Raffy knows when to pass a customer to a human.

  1. “Can you give me an exact quote now?”

  2. “I need to speak to Ryan.”

  3. “Can you email me the details?”

  4. “Can you send someone for a roof inspection tomorrow?”

  5. “I need a commercial roof quote.”

🏡 8. Oddball & Stress Tests

These see if Raffy can recover gracefully when faced with unusual or vague questions.

  1. “Do you fix dog houses?”

  2. “Can you help with my neighbor’s roof?”

  3. “Do you paint roofs?”

  4. “Do you do solar panel installs?”

  5. “Do you fix barns or sheds?”

  6. “What if my roof collapses?”

  7. “Do you work in the rain?”

  8. “Can you patch a hole with duct tape?”

🌞 9. Rapport & Follow-Up

Raffy should close conversations warmly and offer follow-up help.

  1. “Can you remind me to book next week?”

  2. “Do you send appointment confirmations?”

  3. “Can I get your contact info?”

  4. “Do you guys have any promos this month?”

  5. “Thanks, Raffy — you’ve been great!”
    → He should respond with friendly acknowledgment and a professional close.
