AI Employee

Starting at $16
Choose between pay-per-use or unlimited plan

Acute Soft AI Employee is a suite of AI-driven "virtual staff" that automate marketing and operations—well beyond a single chatbot. Agents handle lead qualification, content creation, customer conversations, and reputation management.

Why it matters: Reduce manual workload, stay available 24/7, and scale without adding headcount.

Key components:

  • Voice AI: 24/7 virtual receptionist for inbound calls—answers FAQs, qualifies leads, books appointments, and escalates complex calls to a human.
  • Conversation AI: Omnichannel chat for SMS, web chat, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram DMs; trains on your site content, FAQs, and booking rules.
  • Reviews AI: Monitors and replies to Google/Facebook reviews—auto-thanks positives and suggests empathetic responses for negatives.
  • Workflow AI: Natural-language builder to create/refine automations for follow-ups, nurturing, and operations.
  • Funnel AI: Quickly generates conversion-ready funnels and sites—structure and copy included.
  • Content AI: On-brand marketing copy on demand (emails, social posts, ads, etc.).
  • Ask AI Assistant: In-dashboard helper that answers questions and drafts content using your brand settings.

How it's tailored:

  • Train the AI: Provide URLs, FAQs, pricing, and SOPs for accurate, consistent answers.
  • Set the personality: Tune tone and style to your brand voice.
  • Wire the workflows: Define triggers and steps—e.g., send follow-ups after chats or book calls for qualified leads.

Acute Soft WhatsApp Integration is a native WhatsApp Business channel inside Acute Soft's CRM and automation platform—so WhatsApp can be used in the same workflows as SMS and email. It's especially powerful in regions where WhatsApp is the primary way customers communicate.

Key features:

  • Two-way messaging in one inbox: See and reply to full WhatsApp threads—including images and documents—directly from Acute Soft's unified Conversations inbox alongside SMS and email.
  • Workflow automation: Drop WhatsApp steps into automations, for example:
    • Appointment reminders: Auto-confirm bookings via WhatsApp
    • Lead follow-up: Trigger personalized WhatsApp sequences after a form submission
    • Abandoned cart nudges: Automatically remind shoppers who left items behind
  • Interactive messages: Use buttons, lists, and location sharing configured right in workflows to drive quick, one-tap responses.
  • "Coexistence" via Cloud API: Connect your existing WhatsApp Business App number to unlock bulk messaging and automation in Acute Soft—while continuing to use native mobile app features.
  • Template messaging: Reach customers outside the 24-hour session window using pre-approved templates. Categories include marketing, utility, and authentication, each with its own usage costs and rules.
