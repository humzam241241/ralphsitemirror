Popular Questions?

Get Answers to Your Questions

From pricing to materials, here's everything you need to know to make an informed decision about your roof.

How does Ryan's Roofing use AI in roofing projects?▼
We use AI tools for accurate estimating, drone inspections, and predictive maintenance alerts. This means faster service, fewer surprises, and roofs that last longer.
Do you serve both homes and businesses?▼
Yes. We provide full-service roofing for residential, commercial, and industrial properties throughout Durham Region and the Greater Toronto Area.
Are drone inspections safe and reliable?▼
Absolutely. Our drones provide precise visual data without risking safety on steep or damaged roofs, allowing for faster assessments and better accuracy.
Are you licensed and insured?▼
Absolutely. Ryan's Roofing is fully licensed, insured, and compliant with all Durham area regulations.
How long does a roof replacement take?▼
Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days, depending on size and weather.
What roofing materials do you use?▼
We work with top-rated materials from trusted brands to ensure long-term durability and curb appeal.
What warranties do you offer?▼
All installations are backed by premium manufacturer warranties, plus Ryan's Roofing's own workmanship guarantee.
What if I have an emergency leak?▼
We offer emergency response for leaks and storm damage. Call or text right away — we'll prioritize urgent repairs.
Do you clean up after the job?▼
Always. We leave your yard and driveway spotless, magnet-sweeping for nails and hauling away all debris.
